What is the Unarmed Security D Course?

The Unarmed Security D Course is a state-mandated training program in Florida, designed for individuals who want to work as unarmed security officers. This course covers a broad range of topics, including legal responsibilities, emergency procedures, and best practices for maintaining safety in various environments.

Key Topics Covered in the Course

  • Legal Aspects: Understanding laws related to security work, including detention procedures and the appropriate use of force.
  • Emergency Response: Training in fire safety, medical emergencies, and evacuation protocols.
  • Professional Conduct: Learning the ethics and responsibilities of a security officer.
  • Observation Skills: Developing keen awareness and reporting abilities.
